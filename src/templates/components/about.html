About

Purpose

Gematria Trainer is a free flashcard app for practicing Hebrew Gematria. It uses a spaced-repetition algorithm to schedule reviews at optimal intervals, helping you learn the numerical values and cipher mappings of the Hebrew letters efficiently.

Privacy

This app has no user accounts, no tracking, and no data collection. All your progress and settings are stored locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server.

About this project

Built for personal use and shared for others who want to practice gematria.
